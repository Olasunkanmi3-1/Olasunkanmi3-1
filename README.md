 ## 👨‍💻 About Me

I’m a Cybersecurity Analyst with a strong focus on offensive security and a growing interest in red team operations.

My journey into cybersecurity started with a curiosity about how systems are broken, bypassed, and ultimately secured. Since then, I’ve been building hands-on experience through labs, real-world simulations, and structured learning—focusing on areas like reconnaissance, network analysis, and vulnerability discovery.

I currently hold certifications including CompTIA Security+, ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity, and Cisco CyberOps Associate, and I’m actively advancing my skills toward practical ethical hacking and red teaming.

What sets me apart is my approach: I don’t just use tools. I focus on understanding the logic behind attacks. From analyzing network traffic in Wireshark to performing OSINT-based reconnaissance and documenting findings, I aim to think like an attacker while building like a defender.

Outside of cybersecurity, I have a background in Logistics Operations and leadership, which has helped me develop discipline, structure, and problem-solving skills that I bring into my technical work.

Right now, I’m focused on:
- Deepening my skills in penetration testing and red team methodologies  
- Building and documenting hands-on cybersecurity labs  
- Developing Python scripting for automation and offensive security  
- Creating a strong, practical cybersecurity portfolio  

I’m always open to connecting with professionals in the field and learning from real-world experiences.
